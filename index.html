About US Cargo

US Cargo is a US based shipping company that has the security of your cargo insured through insurance and the best security 

US Cargo was founded in 2021 by James Matthia and was intended to provide quick and easy transportation for businesses and individuals

We are the fastest growing cargo company in the United States

ECO-FRIENDLY

US Cargo has been committed to donating to Geo-friendly organizations from its first days. It is involved in trash removal and research to reduce pollution in the cargo industry, but also in other industries.
